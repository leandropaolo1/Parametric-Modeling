1. True or False. The Exact method can be used to solve $y' + 1/(2ty) = 0$. 
Answer: True. 
partial t = 2y$. While not "immediately" exact, an integrating factor $mu(t,y)$ can typically be found to make such first-order equations exact.

2. True or False. The Separable method can be used to solve $y' + 1/(2ty) = 0$. 
Answer: True. 
dt = -1/(2ty)$. Multiplying gives $y , dy = -1/(2t) , dt$. Since $y$ and $t$ are isolated on opposite sides, it is directly separable.
